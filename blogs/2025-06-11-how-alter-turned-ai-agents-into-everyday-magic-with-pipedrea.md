For Alter to deliver on its promise, it needed to solve one major challenge: connecting everything, fast.</p><h2 id="the-problem">The Problem</h2><p>From the outset, Alter&apos;s product vision hinged on integration. But as a lean startup, they didn&#x2019;t have the time or engineering bandwidth to build and maintain dozens of integrations in-house.</p><blockquote>&#x201c;We needed to launch fast, and with real utility. Building OAuth flows and managing API keys wasn&#x2019;t realistic. And most integration tools weren&#x2019;t built with end users in mind. They made everything harder.&#x201d; <strong>- Olivier Legris, Co-Founder of Alter</strong></blockquote><p>The team evaluated other solutions, including super app backends and low-code platforms. But these introduced friction and felt bolted on&#x2014;counter to the seamless experience Alter wanted to provide. None offered the invisible, scalable architecture they were looking for.</p><p>Ultimately, building the integrations themselves wasn&#x2019;t viable. The time cost was too high, and the resources required would pull the team away from core product development.</p><h2 id="the-solution">The Solution</h2><p>Alter adopted Pipedream Connect&#x2019;s MCP Server to handle its integration layer. Within just two weeks, they had access to hundreds of applications&#x2014;without writing bespoke code or handling the complexity of third-party APIs.</p><blockquote>&#x201c;We launched the same day Pipedream released MCP on Product Hunt. It was the perfect match. You made integration magic possible&#x2014;we made it tangible for users.&#x201d;</blockquote><p>With Pipedream Connect, Alter:</p><ul><li>Instantly unlocked 2,000+ apps</li><li>Avoided months of engineering work</li><li>Delivered real functionality without exposing backend complexity</li></ul><p>Suddenly, users could ask AI agents to fetch analytics, send emails, post updates in Discord, and more&#x2014;all without writing code or managing authentication.</p><p>The value of integrating Pipedream Connect was immediate. They&#x2019;re part of the core experience.&#x201d;</blockquote><p>More importantly, the Alter team could redirect their focus from infrastructure to innovation.
