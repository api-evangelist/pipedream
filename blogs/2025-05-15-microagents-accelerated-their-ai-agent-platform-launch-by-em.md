<img alt="Microagents accelerated their AI agent platform launch by embedding Pipedream&#x2019;s integrations" src="https://pipedream.com/blog/content/images/2025/05/ma1.png" /><p><strong>Irshad Nilam</strong><br />Founder &amp; CEO at <a href="https://microagents.io/?ref=pipedream.com">Microagents</a></p><h3 id="about-the-company">About the Company</h3><p><a href="https://microagents.io/?ref=pipedream.com">Microagents</a> is building a new way to orchestrate AI. The platform allows users to group together specialized AI agents&#x2014;each with domain-specific knowledge and access to different tools&#x2014;and assign them complex tasks. These agents work collaboratively, leveraging integrations with third-party services to perform actions and automate workflows on the user&#x2019;s behalf.</p><p>This modular approach to AI agent collaboration depends on the ability to connect seamlessly with external software. Each integration isn&#x2019;t just a backend connector&#x2014;it represents a functional extension of what each agent can do.</p><h3 id="problem">Problem</h3><p>From the start, the core challenge was scale. To make Microagents useful out of the box, the team needed integrations with a wide range of services&#x2014;CRM tools, communication platforms, databases, and more. But as an early-stage startup, building and maintaining those integrations in-house wasn&#x2019;t realistic.</p><p>Without integrations, the agents couldn&#x2019;t perform meaningful work. But wiring up OAuth flows, maintaining APIs, and registering apps with every service provider would have taken months&#x2014;time the team didn&#x2019;t have. Microagents needed to get to market quickly to validate demand and iterate toward product-market fit.</p><h3 id="solution">Solution</h3><p><a href="https://pipedream.com/connect?ref=pipedream.com">Pipedream</a> provided the infrastructure to make that leap. By using Pipedream Connect as an <a href="https://pipedream.com/docs/connect/mcp/?ref=pipedream.com">MCP server</a>, Microagents enabled their agents to call external APIs securely and reliably. Pipedream&#x2019;s managed authentication eliminated the need to build secure OAuth flows from scratch, while its library of over 2,500 apps meant agents could immediately act on behalf of users across a wide ecosystem.</p><p>Implementation was fast. While the broader platform took time to build, spinning up the MCP servers with Pipedream was straightforward. Compared to other integration platforms like Composio, Pipedream&#x2019;s wider coverage of apps combined with higher quality tools made it the better fit for Microagents&#x2019; needs.</p><p>This foundation let the team focus on building the agent collaboration layer without getting bogged down in integration plumbing. With Pipedream handling the connections, Microagents launched a more powerful product faster&#x2014;and positioned themselves to scale as user demand grows.</p>
